Three spec files contain expectations that no longer match the bot's actual responses:

- **`tests/specs/start.json`**: expects `/start` → `"Welcome! I am ready to help."` (no keyboard), but bot replies with `"Welcome to AGNTDev Bot! I am your assistant..."` + inline keyboard.
- **`tests/specs/T01.json`**: same `/start` mismatch, plus plain-text expects `"I received your message."` but bot replies `"Use /tip for a kind tip..."`.
- **`tests/specs/T03.json`**: `/help` expects 2 commands listed but bot now lists 4; unknown-command expects `"Sorry, I don't recognize that command..."` but bot replies with the generic tip fallback; plain-text expects `"I received your message."` but bot replies differently.

The CI gate globs `tests/specs/*.json` and runs all specs cumulatively. Because the isSubset matcher (`runner.ts:30-31`) uses strict `===` for strings, partial text matching is not supported. These 3 files add ~6 failing specs to the suite, so the gate cannot pass.
